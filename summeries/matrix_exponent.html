A STABLE ALGORITHM FOR MATRIX EXPONENT CALCULATION

Abstract

This article proposes a numerical algorithm for the matrix exponent A(m) = e^M calculation, which is stable for every square complex matrix M and for given natural number k of significant digits. The algorithm is based on C. Lanczos method of eigenvalues calculation. A theoretical analysis and proof of stability of the algorithm is given.
