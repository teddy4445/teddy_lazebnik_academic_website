Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Tolkien Project, part of the MAVERICK research group at the Computer Science Department, Bar Ilan University; Supervised by Prof. Gal A. Kaminka and Dr. Chana Weitman. In addition, a Co-founder (and Algorithm developer) of DnC-algo an algoritmic outsource company.

My research focus on medical and biological nanoparticles (focusing on nanorobotics). Spesificly, modeling a curing protocol based on bio-physical interactions of targeted drug delivery nanoparticles in the blood. I am also intrested in numerical solving and stabalization of ODE and PDE systems (especially, ones originated in a biological setup).

I completed my B.Sc. (2016) and M.Sc. (2018) in Applied Mathematics in Bar-Ilan University.
